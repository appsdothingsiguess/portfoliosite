"Impact of Intermittent Reinforcement Schedules on Task Persistence"

Designed experiment using E-Prime to simulate variable ratio schedules. Managed recruitment for 75 participants.

View Methodology & Findings Hide details
Tools
E-Prime SPSS

Methodology

Utilized a 2x2 factorial design. Participants engaged in a computerized task mimicking slot-machine logic. Reaction times and persistence (number of trials) were logged.

Key Findings

  • Variable Ratio (VR) schedules showed 40% higher persistence than Fixed Ratio (FR).
  • Significant interaction found between reward magnitude and frequency (p < .05).
